\vspace*{0.5cm}
Let $\by$ be an $n \times 1$ vector, $\bX$ be an $n \times K$ matrix, $\bbeta$ is a $K \times 1$ vector of parameters, and $\beps$ is $n \times 1$ vector of errors.  We write a regression equation in the vector form as follows
\begin{align*}
\by=\bX\beta+\beps.
\end{align*}
\begin{enumerate}
\item{Write down the linear regression equation for $\by'$. }
\item{What are the numbers of rows and columns of $\bX'\by$?}
\item{Let $\bX=[\bx_{1}, \bx_{2}, \dots, \bx_{n}]'$ where $\bx_{i}$ is a $K \times 1$ vector and the $i^{th}$ row of $\bX$. Write down $\bX'y$ in terms of $\bx_{i}$ and $y_{i}$ where $y_{i}$ is the $i^{th}$ element of $\by$.}
\item{Is $\bX'\by$ invertible?}
\item{What are the numbers of rows and columns of $\bX'\bX$?}
\item{What are the numbers of rows and columns of $\bX\bX'$?}
\item{Suppose that $\bX$ is full column rank.}
\begin{enumerate}
\item{What is the rank of $\bX'\bX$?}
\item{What is the rank of $\bX\bX'$?}
\item{Is $\bX'\bX$ invertible?}
\item{Is $\bX\bX'$ invertible?}
\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
